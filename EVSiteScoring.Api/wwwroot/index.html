EV Site Scoring

Draw your candidate area, drop a target marker and let EVSS crunch the data.

Workflow

  1. Draw a polygon covering the candidate catchment area.
  2. Click inside the polygon to place the target point.
  3. Adjust optional weights then click Calculate Score.

Weight Overrides

Weights are re-normalised server-side; no need to sum to 1.
